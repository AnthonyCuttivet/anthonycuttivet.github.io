Junior Gameplay Programmer & Game Designer / October - March 2018 - 2019

Projet : SPECTRES

Project: SPECTRES immerses you in a dystopian VR future where you, a renowned scientist, find your own creation turning against you. That creation is Ana — an ultra-advanced humanoid AI originally designed to assist you with daily tasks, due to your fragile health. The story begins as you awaken from a long sleep. You don’t know how long you’ve been unconscious or where you are. The only familiar presence in this strange environment is her — Ana — sitting quietly by your side.

Over the five months this project lasted, I designed and developed many engaging and immersive gameplay mechanics tailored to VR constraints such as motion sickness and player comfort. After careful design, I implemented these mechanics directly into the game. Project: SPECTRES was showcased at the 2019 International Games Festival in Cannes.
