Lead Gameplay Programmer & Game Designer / Global Game Jam 2020

ReParsec

ReParsec is a 1v1 real-time spaceship combat game. Players must constantly choose between attacking their opponent and repairing the damage they’ve sustained. Every action requires performing a random sequence of 8 key inputs, with the effectiveness of the action depending on the precision of your execution. The game is set in a vibrant Retrowave atmosphere.

The 48-hour development sprint for ReParsec really put me to the test and pushed me to focus on what mattered most. I developed the health systems, environment animations, and the core gameplay logic, before moving on to the integration of 2D and 3D assets. Working alongside amazing teammates, we completed the core development in just under 15 hours, which allowed us to dedicate the rest of the time to polishing the Game Feel — an aspect of development I’m especially passionate about.
