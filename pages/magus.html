Magus Academy

Magus Academy is a student party game that blends classic genre elements with fresh new mechanics. The game is set in a magical school during the millennium festival, where the best students from academies worldwide compete for one goal: glory. Take on the role of a prodigious student and outplay your opponents through a variety of challenging trials. Be brave, clever, and cunning to emerge victorious! But beware—the audience is quick to pick their favorite and can grant them powerful advantages through an ancient magic known as Twitch

For Magus Academy, I took on the role of Lead Developer. I planned and developed the game and its features while coordinating task distribution among the development team. My main focus was on designing and implementing gameplay mechanics, integrating the Twitch API into the project, and developing interactive features accessible through the Magus Academy Twitch channel chat.
