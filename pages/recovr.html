Junior Gameplay Programmer / April - October 2020

RecovR

The first module of RecovR takes you into a playful 3D world where you can experience workplace sexism and its consequences. Presented in 15-minutes episodes, it allows you to explore the complexities of this topic and learn how to identify sexist behaviors in order to respond to them more effectively

During these six months as a Junior Gameplay Programmer, I contributed to a wide variety of tasks — from prototyping and implementing gameplay mechanics to integrating UI elements. The area I focused on most was enhancing the Game Feel and helping integrate it into our studio’s workflow. Working closely with designers and artists, I iterated on several elements to ensure a polished and satisfying player experience. This included creating and refining UI animations, designing spell systems, adding easing effects, and more.

You’ll find selected highlights, videos, and a gallery of my work at the bottom of the page.
